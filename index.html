Education

I'm Joelle Yang, and I am currrently a first year at University of California, Riverside studying education. My professional goal is to become an elementary school teacher so that I can help young children develop their skills as they journey through school.

My love for working with elementary school students through teaching stems from the experiences I've had starting from high school. Starting from late middle school, I have volunteered at my church as a Teaching Assistant and often an instructor as well for second graders, and currently still help out at Children's Ministry. Furthermore, I've been tutoring elementary to middle school students in ELA all throughout high school to now. With my students, I help them build skills for reading comprehension along with story and essay writing, teach grammar and vocabulary, and try to prepare them for their next year.

Other informal experiences I have had with teaching comes from teaching my sister and other family friends with what they are learning in school and aiding them with essay writing.
